Lemonade

Description

Lemonade is a colourless sweet fizzy drink. A drink that is made from lemons, sugar,

and water and is not fizzy can also be referred to as lemonade

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar (can reduce to 3/4 cup)
  • 1 cup water (for the simple syrup)
  • 1 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 2 to 3 cups cold water, to dilute

Steps

  1. Place the sugar and water in a small saucepan and bring to a simmer. Stir so that the sugar dissolves completely and remove from heat.
  2. While the water is heating for the simple syrup, juice your lemons. Depending on the size of the lemons, 4 to 6 of them should be enough for 1 cup of juice.
  3. Pour the juice and the simple syrup sugar water into a serving pitcher. Add 2 to 3 cups of cold water and taste.
  4. Refrigerate 30 to 40 minutes.Serve with ice and sliced lemons.
